From raw draft to
compliant manuscript.

GenScholar combines large language models with format-specific rule engines to give publication-ready output.

"Manual reformatting consumes 20–30% of a researcher's time, leading to delayed submissions and high desk rejection rates due to formatting errors."

— Motivation for GenScholar
01
Paste your draft
Upload any text — no specific structure required. Include your title, abstract, body, and references in any order.
02
Select a format
Choose from IEEE, APA, Springer, ACM, Scopus, or Elsevier. Each has distinct rules the AI knows precisely.
03
AI restructures it
Google Gemini parses the semantic structure — sections, citations, abstract — and reformats everything to spec.
04
Review issues
A compliance report flags every rule violation — errors that cause desk rejection, warnings, and what's correct.
05
Analyze deeper
Check readability scores, abstract structure, citation validity, and word/page count compliance automatically.
06
Copy & submit
Copy the formatted output, fix flagged issues, and submit with confidence. No more desk rejections for formatting.
